Record Outlaw

About

"Record Outlaw" is a gripping crime thriller that follows the life of a skilled hacker turned vigilante, who uses his unparalleled technological expertise to expose corrupt politicians and bring them to justice, all while staying one step ahead of the law. With its pulse-pounding action sequences and a captivating storyline, this film delves into the dark underbelly of power and showcases the extraordinary lengths one man will go to fight for truth and redemption.
